# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 7, 2026**

## Overview

Lenguas ("we", "our", or "us") is a language learning app that helps users practice vocabulary and pronunciation. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

## Information We Collect

### Microphone Access

Lenguas requests access to your device's microphone solely to enable pronunciation practice. When you record yourself speaking:

- Audio is captured on your device and sent to our server for pronunciation assessment.
- Audio recordings are processed in real time and are **not stored** after assessment is complete.
- We do not use your voice data for any purpose other than evaluating your pronunciation within the app.

### Learning Progress

We store your vocabulary practice progress (words seen, correct/incorrect answers) on our servers to enable spaced repetition and track your improvement over time. This data is associated with your account and is not shared with third parties.

## Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers unless you create an account.
- We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or third parties.
- We do not use your microphone in the background or at any time other than when you initiate a pronunciation exercise.

## Data Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Audio data is discarded immediately after processing and is never written to permanent storage.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
